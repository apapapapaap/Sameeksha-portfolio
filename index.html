About Me

My Musical Journey

A Multi-Genre Singer Bringing Melody to Every Stage

Music has been a part of me since childhood. Watching singers on TV and my first performance in 3rd grade sparked a passion that has stayed with me.

Through school performances and Hindustani classical training, music remained constant even when academics interrupted my lessons.

I'm drawn to folk, semi-classical, and soft romantic melodies, and I also sing retro classics, pop, energetic songs and fusion.

I primarily sing in Kannada, Hindi, and English, and I'm now exploring Tamil and Telugu.
